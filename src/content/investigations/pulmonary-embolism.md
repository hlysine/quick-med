# Pulmonary Embolism (ECG)

![Pulmonary embolism ECG](/assets/pulmonary-embolism-ecg.png)

* May have normal ECG
* Complete/incomplete RBBB
* Right ventricle strain: Anterior ST changes and **T inversion**
* **S1Q3T3**: S in I, Q and T in III
    * Right ventricular strain pattern
    * Also in cor pulmonale
* ECG not suitable to diagnose/exclude PE
