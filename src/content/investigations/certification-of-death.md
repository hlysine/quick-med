# Certification of Death

* Check and document the following (*attach ECG/cardiac monitor rhythm strip to progress notes*):
    * Pupils dilated and fixed
    * Blood pressure not measurable
    * Pulse not palpable
    * Heart sounds not auscultable
    * Breath sounds not auscultable/no spontaneous breathing, no chest rise
    * ECG flat line
    * Patient certified on xx/xx/xxxx at xx:xx (use the time printed on ECG/rhythm strip as the official time of certified dead)
    * Cat 1 body (Cat 2 or 3 if infectious; see below)

## Pacemaker/ICD removal
* **Inactivate defibrillator with magnet**, wear double gloves before making incision
* Remove pacemaker/ICD (/port-a-cath): make a ~2-3cm incision superficial to the device and dissect fibrous tissue until you can squeeze the device out; then **cut the wires**, put the device into a specimen bag, and close the wound (simple interrupted suture x1 is fine)
* **NO NEED to remove the wires connecting the device to the heart!** Dissecting them into the large vessels is tedious and will cause massive bleeding.
