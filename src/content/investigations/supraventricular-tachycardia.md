# Supraventricular Tachycardia (SVT)

![SVT ECG](/assets/svt-ecg.png)

## Atrioventricular nodal reentrant tachycardia (AVNRT)

![AVNRT ECG](/assets/avnrt-ecg.png)

* Circular circuit within AV node
* Both atrial and ventricular contraction but with abnormal synchronization
* Fast and regular beat
* Inverted P wave may be visible in inferior leads
    * Retrograde atrial depolarization

## Atrioventricular Reentrant Tachycardia (AVRT) / Accessory Pathway

### AV accessory pathway - Preexcitation

![WPW pre-excitation delta wave](/assets/wpw-preexcitation-delta-wave.png)

* Early anterograde activation of ventricles via accessory pathway
* **Delta wave**
    * Up-slanting of QR segment
    * Because of slow muscle-to-muscle conduction after accessory pathway
* Position of delta wave allows for determination of accessory pathway location (advanced)

### AV Reentrant Tachycardia (AVRT)

* Retrograde conduction in accessory pathway
* Circular circuit with AV node conduction
* Atrial contraction present

#### Orthodromic AVRT

![Orthodromic AVRT ECG](/assets/avrt-orthodromic-ecg.png)

* **Narrow QRS**
* Down in AVN, up in accessory pathway
* Retrograde inverted P wave after QRS
* Similar ECG to AVNRT

#### Antidromic AVRT

![Antidromic AVRT ECG](/assets/avrt-antidromic-ecg.png)

* **Wide QRS**
* Down in accessory pathway, up in AVN
* Delta waves present
* Similar ECG to ventricular tachycardia

### Wolff-Parkinson-White pattern

Pre-excitation on ECG without symptoms.

### Wolff-Parkinson-White syndrome

Pre-excitation on ECG with documented tachyarrhythmia.
