# Non-ST Elevation Acute Coronary Syndrome (NSTE-ACS)

## Unstable angina

### Presentation

* New onset ischemic chest pain
* Angina at rest
* Varying intensity, duration and provocation

* ST depression
* T wave abnormalities
* No cardiac troponin enzyme elevation

## Non-ST Elevation Myocardial Infarction (NSTEMI)

![NSTEMI ECG 1](/assets/nstemi-ecg-1.png)

![NSTEMI ECG 2](/assets/nstemi-ecg-2.png)

> **1/3 of NSTEMI have normal ECG**
>
> Cannot rule out NSTEMI.
>
> Always repeat ECG after 15-30 minutes.

* **ST elevation:** Absent
* **ST depression:** Downsloping/horizontal ST depression in 2 leads of the same area
* **T inversion**/biphasic + R/S>1 in 2 leads of the same area
* **Location of infarct does not correlate to lead changes**
* Confirm diagnosis with **cardiac troponin enzyme elevation**
