# Atrial Premature Complex (APC)

![Atrial premature complex](/assets/atrial-premature-complex.png)

* P wave shape different than normal
    * May be buried in T wave of preceding beat
* PR interval different than normal
* RR interval not regular
* Wide QRS if beat is very premature
* APC may be blocked if AVN is still refractory
