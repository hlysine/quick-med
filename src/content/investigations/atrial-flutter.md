# Atrial Flutter (AFL)

![Atrial flutter ECG](/assets/atrial-flutter-ecg.png)

* Organized fast atrial activity
* Fast and regular beat
* **Sawtooth flutter** waves in II, III, aVF
* Flutter wave conducts to ventricles at fixed ratio, e.g. 4:1
* Regular QRS complex
