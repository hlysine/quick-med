# Ventricular Premature Complex (VPC)

![Ventricular premature complex](/assets/ventricular-premature-complex.png)

* Follows a normal beat
* Followed by a pause that skips a beat but maintains the RR interval multiple
* Wide QRS
* No preceding P wave
* May have polymorphic appearance if complexes originate from different foci
* Ventricular couplets → ventricular triplets → ventricular tachycardia
* Fusion complex: sinus and ventricular complex merge
