# Certifying death

- Pupils fixed and dilated
- Blood pressure not measurable
- Pulse not palpable
- Heart sounds not audible
- No respiratory effort
- ECG flat line
- Pacemaker/ICD removed

Certify time of death according to ECG report.

## Body risk category

- Cat 1 - Regular
- Cat 2 - HIV, Hep C, COVID-19
- Cat 3 - Anthrax, Plague, Rabies, CJD with necropsy
