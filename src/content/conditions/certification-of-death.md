# Certification of Death

## Assessment

Check and document all of the following:

- Pupils dilated and fixed
- BP not measurable
- Pulse not palpable
- Heart sounds not auscultable
- Breath sounds not auscultable / no spontaneous breathing / no chest rise
- ECG flat line

## Management

### Documentation

- Time of death = time printed on ECG / rhythm strip

### Body Category

- Cat 1 body by default
- Cat 2 or Cat 3 if infectious

### Pacemaker / ICD Removal

- Inactivate defibrillator with magnet prior to removal
- Wear double gloves
- Make ~2-3cm incision over device pocket
- Remove device and cut wires
- No need to remove wires going into the heart
