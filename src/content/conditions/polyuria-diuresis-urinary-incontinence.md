# Polyuria / Diuresis / Urinary Incontinence

## Causes
* **Polyuria/diuresis: diuretics, osmotic (DM), UTI**, polydipsia, cranial/nephrogenic DI, channelopathies
* **Urinary incontinence:** multiparity, detrusor overactivity, neurogenic bladder, **overflow incontinence**

## Management
* **Polyuria/diuresis:**
    * Chart I/O
    * Fluid replacement (for post-obstructive diuresis: replace half to 2/3 of lost volume)
    * Monitor RFT, electrolytes, H'stix
    * FG, A1c x1
    * MSU/CSU x c/st
    * If persistent: paired serum/urine osmo and Na, refer MED x workup
* **Urinary incontinence: r/o neurological cause**, pelvic floor exercise, refer URO/GYN if needed

> **Pitfall:** chronic retention of urine may present as overflow incontinence (PVRU will still be >350ml); in these cases, **anticholinergics are C/I!**
