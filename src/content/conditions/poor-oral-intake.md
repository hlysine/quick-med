# Poor Oral Intake

> **No strict definition, but nurses may inform you if intake <300 ml/day or <1/3 of meal**

## Causes
* Poor appetite: infection, cancer, pain, SOB etc.
* GI: **dysphagia, N/V/abd pain**, tooth loss, xerostomia
* Endocrine/metabolic: ↑ Ca, ↓ thyroid, ↓ cortisol, ESKD, ESLD
* Psychiatric: dementia, AN, mood/psychotic disorders

> **Diet types:**
> * **Surgical post-op diet upgrade:** F/D (fluid diet 流餐) -> congee diet 粥餐 -> S/D (soft diet) 軟餐 -> DAT
> * **Puree diet 糊餐** of different consistencies prescribed by speech therapist for swallowing problems
> * **Medical diets:** DM, low salt, renal, warfarin, low purine, neutropenic

## Management
* **If dysphagia:** NPOEM, consult speech therapist
* Encourage oral intake, **consult dietitian** x poor oral intake
* Chart I/O, H'stix BD
* **IVF Q12H or Q8H (to prevent dehydration/AKI)**
* **Treat underlying cause** (can just briefly assess for GI causes)
