## Definition

- Nurses may inform if intake **<300ml/day** or **<1/3 of meal**

## Causes

| Category | Examples |
|---|---|
| Poor appetite | Infection, cancer, pain, SOB |
| GI | Dysphagia, N/V, abdominal pain, tooth loss, xerostomia |
| Endocrine/metabolic | Hypercalcaemia, thyroid disease, cortisol deficiency, ESKD, ESLD |
| Psychiatric | Dementia, anorexia nervosa, mood/psychotic disorders |

## Management

- If **dysphagia**: NPOEM, consult **Speech Therapist**
- Encourage oral intake; consult **Dietitian**
- Chart I/O, H'stix BD
- **IVF Q12H or Q8H** (to prevent dehydration/AKI)
- Treat underlying cause
