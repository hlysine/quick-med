# Numbness

## Causes
* **Brain: HI, CVA (ischaemic/haemorrhagic), SOL (haematoma/abscess/tumour)**, encephalitis, demyelination, seizure/post-ictal
* **Spinal cord:** **trauma, CVA, myelopathy** (haematoma/abscess/tumour/spondylosis/OPLL), transverse myelitis (demyelination, autoimmune diseases, infection, ADEM, paraneoplastic), B12 deficiency
* Nerve root: cauda equina, radiculopathy (PID, tumour etc.)
* Neuropathy: hyperventilation, hypoCa, **DM, alcoholic**, B1/B6/B12 deficiency, hypothyroid, CKD, CLD, drugs, autoimmune, **paraneoplastic**, MM, lymphoma, HIV, Lyme disease, GBS, CIDP, CMT, nerve entrapment/trauma

## Assessment
* Onset, trigger (e.g. cycler's/Saturday night palsy)
* **Pattern:** perioral (LA systemic toxicity) vs face (trigeminal) vs sensory level (cord) vs dermatomal (nerve root) vs peripheral nerve (mononeuropathy) vs glove and stocking (polyneuropathy)
* **Associated symptoms:** weakness, headache/**slurring** (brain), sphincter disturbance (cord/nerve root)
* **Full neuro exam:** CN (pupils, EOM, CN7, dysarthria), 4 limbs **power/reflex/sensation**, cerebellar signs
* If suspect **cord pathology:** spinal tenderness, **straight leg raise, PR** for tone, grip, perianal sensation

## Management
* Brain/cord imaging and neuro-obs if suspect CNS pathology
* Bld x FG, A1c, +/- TFT, B12
* **Neurobion** 1 tab daily
* Neuropathic pain: Lyrica/gabapentin
* NCS, consult/refer neuro if unexplained
