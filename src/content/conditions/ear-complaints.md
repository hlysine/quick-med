## Assessment

- **External ear**: pain, discharge, bleeding, blocked ear; inspection + otoscopy
- **Middle ear**: bulging erythematous tympanic membrane on otoscopy = acute otitis media
- **Inner ear**: vertigo, tinnitus
- **Hearing loss**: conductive vs sensorineural

## Management

- **Consult ENT**
- **Otitis externa**: antibiotic ear drops — ofloxacin, or dexamethasone 0.1% + neomycin 0.5%
- **Acute otitis media**: PO Augmentin (amoxicillin-clavulanate); doxycycline if beta-lactam allergy
- **Earwax**: olive oil ear drops
