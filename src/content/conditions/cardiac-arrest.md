# Cardiac Arrest

## Assessment

- No pulse, no breathing

## Management

### Houseman Role

- Set IV access
- Take arrest bloods
- Houseman leads resuscitation if team is inexperienced

### ACLS Algorithm

- Early recognition of cardiac arrest
- Activate arrest call
- Chest compressions +/- defibrillations per ACLS protocol

### Arrest Bloods

- CBC d/c, clotting, L/RFT, Ca/PO4, ABG, lactate, NH3, RG, TnI/CK +/- T&S, blood c/st
- Blood taking: femoral artery via 20ml syringe

**Bottle colours**

| Colour     | Additive       | Use                         |
|------------|----------------|-----------------------------|
| Blue (x1)  | Na citrate     | Clotting                    |
| Green (x3-4) | Li heparin   | L/RFT, Ca/PO4, lactate, NH3 |
| Purple (x2) | K EDTA        | CBC d/c                     |
| Grey (x2)  | Na fluoride    | RG (glucose)                |

### Post-ROSC Care

- Check vitals
- Cardiac monitoring
- ECG
- CXR
- Continue looking for and treating underlying cause
