## Causes

| Category | Examples |
|---|---|
| **Mechanical** | Vertebral collapse, bone metastasis, PID, spondylosis, facet joint disease |
| **Muscular** | Muscle sprain / spasm |
| **Infective** | Spondylodiscitis |
| **Inflammatory** | Spondyloarthritis |
| **Neuropathic** | Spinal stenosis, cord compression, cauda equina syndrome, radiculopathy |
| **Referred** | AAA, pancreatitis, loin pain |

## Assessment

- **History**: onset (acute / chronic / trauma), radiation, characteristics (mechanical / neuropathic / inflammatory / nocturnal)
- Associated symptoms: fever, weakness, numbness, sphincter disturbance
- **Spine exam**: tenderness, stepping, paraspinal spasm, sensory level
- **Lower limb neurology**: tone, power and reflexes by myotome, sensation by dermatome
- **Sciatica signs**: SLR, crossed SLR, hamstring sign, bowstring sign
- **PR**: tone, grip, sensation
- **Abdominal exam**: tenderness, pulsatile expansile mass (AAA)

## Management

- **Neurological compression signs/symptoms**: urgent MRI C/T/LS spine + whole-spine sagittal screening, **urgent consult neurosurgery**
- **New vertebral collapse**: if stable (compression fracture) → refer P&O for thoracolumbar orthosis
- **Old vertebral collapse**: refer P&O for soft lumbar corset
- **Non-specific**: analgesics, back physiotherapy
- **Neuropathic pain**: Lyrica (pregabalin) / gabapentin
- Refer orthopaedics / rheumatology as appropriate
