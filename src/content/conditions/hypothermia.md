# Hypothermia

> **Usually nurses will inform you when Temp < 36°C**

## Causes
sepsis, hypothyroidism, trauma, anorexia nervosa

## Management
* Septic workup and antibiotic same as fever
* Blair Hugger (*C/I for myxedema coma -> passive warming with blanket instead*)
