# Hypothermia

## Definition

- Temp <36°C

## Causes

- Sepsis
- Hypothyroidism (myxoedema coma)
- Trauma
- Anorexia nervosa

## Management

- Septic workup and antibiotics same as **Fever**
- **Blair Hugger** for active warming
  - **C/I for myxoedema coma** — use passive warming with blanket instead
