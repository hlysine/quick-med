## Symptoms

Cough, sore throat, rhinorrhoea, nasal congestion

## Causes

| Symptom | Causes |
|---|---|
| **Cough** | Lower tract disease (pneumonia, asthma, COPD, ILD, bronchiectasis, TB, lung cancer), URTI (influenza, COVID, common cold) / post-viral / allergic, GERD / aspiration, heart failure, ACEI |
| **Nasal symptoms** | URTI, allergic rhinitis, nasal polyp |

## Assessment

- Symptoms and onset
- Sputum colour if productive
- **Red flags**: SOB, haemoptysis → consider lower tract / cardiac cause

## Management

- **If SOB**: consider lower respiratory tract or cardiac aetiology
- NPS × COVID + influenza ± respiratory virus panel
- Sputum C&ST

### Cough

- **Productive**: Fluimucil / Bisolvon (mucolytic); MES / guaifenesin (expectorant)
- **Dry**: Pholcodine / codeine / dextromethorphan (suppressant)

### Sore Throat

- Dequadin lozenge, Panadol
- Chlorhexidine mouthwash LA 10 ml BD
- Bonjela LA (topical)

### Rhinorrhoea / Nasal Congestion

- **Decongestant**: phenylephrine / pseudoephedrine
- **Allergy**: antihistamine (Piriton / cetirizine / diphenhydramine / promethazine); steroid nasal spray
