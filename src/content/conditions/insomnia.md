## Management

- **PO Piriton (chlorphenamine) 4 mg** nocte PRN
- **Gravol (dimenhydrinate)** — also an antihistamine; may be more effective
- Consult clinical psychologist or psychiatry if persistent
- **Avoid Z-drugs** (e.g. Stilnox/zolpidem) — risk of tolerance and dependence
