# Insomnia

## Management
* PO Piriton 4mg nocte prn
* Gravol (also antihistamine) may be more effective
* Consult clinical psychologist or psychiatry if persistent

> Avoid starting Z drugs like Stilnox due to risk of tolerance and dependence (you are not case MO so will not follow up to make sure it is for short-term use only! E.g. STAT dose instead of regular/ PRN)
