## Causes

- Caffeine, alcohol, drugs, withdrawal
- Arrhythmia
- Hypoglycaemia
- Anaemia
- Sepsis
- Thyrotoxicosis
- Phaeochromocytoma
- Pregnancy / perimenopause
- Anxiety / panic disorder

## Assessment

- **Vitals:** pulse regularity
- Onset, character (fast vs slow, regular vs irregular)
- **Cardiac/anaemic symptoms:** chest discomfort, SOB, dizziness, LOC
- **Autonomic symptoms:** tremor, sweating, heat intolerance, diarrhoea, amenorrhoea
  - DDx: hypoglycaemia / thyrotoxicosis / phaeochromocytoma / climacteric
- Anxiety/panic
- Caffeine/alcohol/drug intake

## Management

- **H'stix monitoring**
- **Cardiac monitor** (especially if true tachycardia)
- **ECG LLII**
- Bloods: CBC + RFT + Ca/PO4 + TFT
  - 24hr urine metanephrines **only if** headache + autonomic symptoms (phaeochromocytoma screen)
- Treat underlying cause
- May need consult/referral to **MED** for arrhythmia/endocrinopathy
