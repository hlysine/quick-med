# Contrast Allergy Premedication

Each hospital has its own protocol, so the following is for reference only! Contact pharmacy if in doubt!

## Elective scans
* PO Prednisolone 40mg 12 hours before scan **AND**
* PO Prednisolone 40mg 2 hours before scan
* +/- PO Piriton

## Emergency scans
* IV Hydrocortisone 200mg STAT then 200mg Q4H until contrast administration
