## Premedication

> Each hospital has its own protocol — refer to local guidelines.

### Elective Scans

- **PO Prednisolone 40 mg** — 12 hours before scan
- **PO Prednisolone 40 mg** — 2 hours before scan
- ± PO Piriton (chlorphenamine)

### Emergency Scans

- **IV Hydrocortisone 200 mg STAT**, then **200 mg Q4H** until contrast administration
