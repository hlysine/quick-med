# Eye Complaints

> Sorry, don't know OVS >< but:

## Assessment
* Symptoms: pain, redness, blurring
* Inspect the eye
* Check VA
* Check pupils, EOM

> **Random facts:**
> * In *Klebsiella pneumoniae* septicaemia, need to routine consult OVS to rule out endophthalmitis
> * Need to chart VA and perform Ishihara colour chart before starting Isoniazid for TB

## Red flags: urgent consult OVS
* Acute **decrease in VA** (e.g. acute angle closure glaucoma: eye pain +/- headache, blurring, see halos)
* **Red eye with pain** (scleritis) / **photophobia** (keratitis/iritis) / **mucopurulent discharge** (bacterial)
* **Hyphema / hypopyon** (visible layer of RBC / WBC in anterior chamber)
* Eye trauma/injury

Otherwise, **routine consult OVS**

*If pupil/EOM abnormalities: consider CNS pathology!!*

> **Tips for consulting OVS:** they will ask you about the VA of each eye!
