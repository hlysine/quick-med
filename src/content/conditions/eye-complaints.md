## Assessment

- Symptoms: pain, redness, blurring
- Inspect the eye
- Check **visual acuity (VA)**
- Check **pupils** and **extraocular movements (EOM)**

## Management

### Red Flags — Urgent Consult Ophthalmology (OVS)

- **Acute VA loss** (e.g. acute angle-closure glaucoma: eye pain ± headache, blurring, seeing halos)
- **Red eye with pain** (scleritis) / **photophobia** (keratitis / iritis) / mucopurulent discharge
- **Hyphema / hypopyon** (visible layer of RBC / WBC in anterior chamber)
- **Eye trauma / injury**

### Routine

- No red flags: routine consult ophthalmology

### CNS Pathology

- If **pupil or EOM abnormalities**: consider CNS pathology
