## Definition

- Nurses may inform if **>50ml/4hrs**

## Causes

- Gastric outlet obstruction (GOO)
- Intestinal obstruction (IO)
- Ileus

## Assessment

- Content of output
- Any vomiting
- **Abdomen:** pain, tenderness, succussion splash
- Signs of **aspiration**

## Management

- **R/T to BSB** (not spigotted), aspirate Q4H
- W/H R/T feeding; maintenance IVF
- Monitor RFT + VBG + Ca/PO4 + Mg
- **AXR** (+ CXR if suspect aspiration)
- Add PPI; off Imodium/opioids
- If **bloody/coffee ground output:** refer to Haematemesis section
