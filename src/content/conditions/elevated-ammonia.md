## Management

- **Neuro-obs**
- Look for underlying factors for worsening liver function:
  - Spontaneous bacterial peritonitis (SBP)
  - Variceal bleeding
  - Infection
- Add / increase laxatives (especially **lactulose**); aim for **BO 2–3 times per day**
