- **Methods to confirm R/T position**:
  - Gurgling sound on air injection
  - Gastric aspirate pH
  - **CXR (gold standard)**

- If R/T position is not correct: **DO NOT START FEEDING**; order to advance (if not deep enough), withdraw (if coiled), or reinsert then repeat CXR; better safe than sorry (aspiration pneumonia)
