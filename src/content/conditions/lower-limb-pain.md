# Lower Limb Pain

Other than joint/bone pain, need to consider:
* **Limb ischaemia** (acute signs: **pain, pallor, poikilothermia, pulselessness, paraesthesia, paralysis**) -> NPOEM, urgent consult vascular
* **DVT (asymmetric calf swelling and tenderness**; risk factors in Virchow's triad) -> urgent USG Doppler
* **Compartment syndrome** (history of fracture, cast, limb ischaemia; **tense compartment, pain on passive stretching**) -> NPOEM, urgent consult ORT
* **Cellulitis** -> mark erythema, antibiotics (e.g. Augmentin)
* **NF (blisters, crepitus)** -> NPOEM, IV broad spectrum antibiotics, urgent consult ORT
* **Projected pain from spine / hip**
