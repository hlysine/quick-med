## Causes

Consider causes beyond joint/bone pain:

### Acute Limb Ischaemia

- **6 Ps**: pain, pallor, poikilothermia, pulselessness, paraesthesia, paralysis
- **Management**: NPOEM, **urgent consult vascular surgery**

### Deep Vein Thrombosis (DVT)

- Asymmetric calf swelling and tenderness; Virchow's triad risk factors
- **Management**: urgent USG Doppler

### Compartment Syndrome

- History of fracture, cast, or limb ischaemia; tense compartment; **pain on passive stretching**
- **Management**: NPOEM, **urgent consult orthopaedics**

### Cellulitis

- **Management**: mark erythema margins; antibiotics (e.g. Augmentin)

### Necrotising Fasciitis

- Blisters, crepitus; **rapidly progressive**
- **Management**: NPOEM, IV broad-spectrum antibiotics, **urgent consult orthopaedics**

### Projected Pain from Spine / Hip

- Consider radiculopathy or referred pain from lumbar spine or hip joint
