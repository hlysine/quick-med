## Treatment

- **PO Oseltamivir (Tamiflu) 75 mg BD × 5 days**
- Extend up to **10 days** if ongoing lower respiratory tract disease

### Renal Dosing

| CrCl | Dose |
|---|---|
| 30–60 | 30 mg BD |
| 10–30 | 30 mg daily |
| <10 | Contraindicated |

## Prophylaxis

- Half the therapeutic dose
- Indicated for **high-risk patients** who are:
  1. Contraindicated to vaccination or expected low vaccine effectiveness, **OR**
  2. Close contact within **48 hours** of a confirmed case
