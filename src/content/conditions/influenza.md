# Influenza (flu) A or B

* **PO Oseltamivir (Tamiflu) 75mg BD x 5 days**
    * Renal dosing: 30mg BD for CrCl 30-60; 30mg daily for CrCl 10-30; C/I for CrCl < 10
    * Extend up to 10 days if ongoing lower tract disease
* PO Oseltamivir **at half the therapeutic dose** can be given as prophylaxis in high-risk patients who are 1) C/I to vaccination / expect low effectiveness of vaccination, or 2) close contact within 48hrs
