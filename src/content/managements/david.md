# DAVID

## Diet

- NPOEM
  - Requires maintenance IVF
  - For DM
    - Withhold DM medications
    - 1/2-1/2 solution
  - For surgery
    - 2D1S Q8h
- 1800kcal DM diet
- Fluid restriction
- Low salt diet - CHF
- Renal diet
- Warfarin diet
- Low purine diet
- Neutropenic diet
- Clear fluid diet - colonoscopy
- Post-op
  - Fluid diet
  - Congee diet
  - Soft diet
- Puree diet - swallowing difficulty

## Activity

- Bed rest
- Fall precaution
- Head down - hypotension / off central line
- Prop up - desaturation / CHF

## Vitals

- BP/P
- Cardiac monitoring
- SpO2 monitoring, keep SpO2 >94%
- Neuro obs
- Chart IO
- Strict IO
- Limb circulation chart
- Motor/sensory chart
